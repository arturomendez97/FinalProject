Welcome to Flow!

This is the new and rising social / learning hub for artists. This is not a portafolio builder. Here you're free to upload any current work in progress or finished art with the intent of getting critique.

Who will give you feedback? Other users! And you as well can comment on other people's art.

Of course, you can also like, favourite, and follow other artists. However, the main page only shows recent artworks and the ones from the people you follow. There's no sort by likes or favorites because this is not a popularity contest, this is for learning.
